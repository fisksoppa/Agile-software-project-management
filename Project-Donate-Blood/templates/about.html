About Us

Making Donating Blood Easy Again

Group Dagobah

We are students in agile software project management, concerned with an ethical future where technology is put to the benefit of humans. Putting our skills at the service of people is to allow us all to have access to health. We have observed that access to blood in the Gothenburg region is not so easy. So we decided to develop our application DonateBlood, which allows us to gather the location of all the places where it is possible to give blood. We are proud of our success and are looking for new challenges in order to live in a pleasant and sustainable world. This project was made to help society move closer to UNs Sustainable Development Goal 3. We are a group of students combined from Chalmers University and Goteborgs Universitet and we want to put our values at the center of our work. Thus we aim to develop ethical software in a productive, healthy and respectful atmosphere.
